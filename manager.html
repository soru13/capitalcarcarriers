ABOUT US

Incorporated in 2006, Capital Car Carriers owner Craig Gilchrist has over 30 years of experience in the industry. With the head office located in North Gower, Ontario - a small community minutes south of Canada's capital, Ottawa - Capital Car Carriers provides reliable, cost effective vehicle transportation services.

Vehicle Shipping for Dealers While able to ship nationwide from your lot to your destination, Capital Car Carriers normally caters to clients within Ontario, Quebec, the Maritimes, and Short US. We provide dealers with excellent vehicle transportation - our reputation speaks for itself!
